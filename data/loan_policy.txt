Home Loan Approval Policy

1. Eligibility Criteria
   - Applicant must be at least 18 years old.
   - Applicant must have a steady source of income.
   - Co-applicant is recommended for higher loan amounts.

2. Credit History
   - A credit history score of 1.0 (Good) is highly preferred.
   - Applicants with a credit history of 0.0 (Bad) are likely to be rejected.

3. Loan Amount and Term
   - Loan amount depends on the applicant's income and repayment capacity.
   - Standard loan term is 360 months (30 years).

4. Property Area
   - Loans are available for Urban, Semiurban, and Rural areas.
   - Semiurban areas often have higher approval rates.

5. Documentation
   - Valid ID proof.
   - Income proof (Salary slips or IT returns).
   - Property documents.

6. Rejection Reasons
   - Low income relative to loan amount.
   - Poor credit history.
   - Incomplete documentation.
